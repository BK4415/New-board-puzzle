How to Play

The Goal

Arrange all numbered tiles (or photo pieces) in the correct order by sliding them into the empty space. The empty square is always in the bottom-right in Classic mode.

Controls

1
Tap a tile

Tap any tile directly adjacent (up, down, left, right) to the empty space. It slides into the gap.

2
Swipe to slide a row or column

Swipe left/right to slide the entire row containing the empty space.
Swipe up/down to slide the entire column. Multiple tiles move at once!

3
Arrow keys (desktop)

Use ← → ↑ ↓ arrow keys to slide tiles in that direction.

Game Modes

🔢
Classic

Numbers 1–8 in left-to-right, top-to-bottom order. Empty at bottom-right.

🐍
Snake

Even rows go left-to-right, odd rows go right-to-left — like a snake path.

🌀
Spiral

Numbers spiral clockwise from the top-left corner inward. The empty space ends up in the center.

🙃
Upside Down

The solved order is reversed — number 1 is in the bottom-right, empty is top-left.

🖼️
Photo Mode

Reassemble a photo cut into tiles. Tap the 👁️ button to toggle number hints on tiles. Only 3×3 grid is supported in Photo mode.

Tips & Tricks

  • Solve corner pieces first, then edges, then center.
  • Use Undo to reverse a bad move — but it counts as an extra move!
  • Shuffle scrambles the board and resets your move counter.
  • In Photo mode, turn numbers OFF for the full challenge.
  • A fast solve unlocks the ⚡ Fast Solver achievement!

Scoring

Your performance is measured by moves and time. Fewer moves and faster times set new records. Records are saved per puzzle type.
