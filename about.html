About Board Puzzle

Board Puzzle is a premium offline sliding puzzle game built with pure HTML5, CSS3, and Vanilla JavaScript — no frameworks, no dependencies. Slide tiles into the correct order to solve each puzzle!

What Makes It Special

  • 4 number modes — Classic, Snake, Spiral, Upside Down
  • Photo mode — Solve puzzles made from your own photos or preset images
  • Multiple grid sizes — 3×3, 4×4, and 5×5
  • Wood & Dark themes — Beautiful premium aesthetics
  • Achievements & stats — Track your progress
  • 100% offline — Works without internet as a PWA

How to Play

Tap a tile adjacent to the empty space to slide it, or swipe across the board to slide an entire row or column. Arrange all tiles in the correct order to win!
