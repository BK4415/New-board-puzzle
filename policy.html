Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Our Commitment

Board Puzzle is designed with privacy at its core. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to any server — ever.

What Data Is Stored

All game data is stored locally on your device only using your browser's localStorage. This includes:

  • Your game preferences (theme, sound, grid size, mode)
  • Your game statistics (wins, best time, best moves, streaks)
  • Achievement progress
  • Your current unfinished game (for the Resume feature)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Photos & Images

When you upload a photo for Photo mode, the image is processed entirely within your browser using the Canvas API. Your photo is never uploaded to any server and is discarded when you close the app.

Network Usage

Board Puzzle is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that works fully offline. After the initial load, no network requests are made. Game assets are cached on your device via the Service Worker.

Third-Party Services

Board Puzzle uses no third-party services, analytics tools, advertising networks, or tracking libraries of any kind.

Children's Privacy

Board Puzzle does not collect any information from anyone, including children. The game is safe for all ages.

Clearing Your Data

You can clear all stored game data at any time by clearing your browser's site data or localStorage for this app. This will reset all stats, achievements, and preferences.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this policy, the new version will be reflected in the app. Since we collect no data, any changes would only concern how the app uses local storage.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please reach out via the app's listing page.
